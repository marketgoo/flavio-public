== Description ==

Flavio: Effortless SEO for Small Businesses

Running a small business is demanding enough without adding complex SEO tasks to your plate. Flavio is the SEO plugin (Agent) designed specifically for non-experts, working intelligently behind the scenes to improve your visibility, fix critical technical issues, and help you show up where your customers are searching.

Key Features and Benefits
- Simple Onboarding: Get your site optimized quickly with our intuitive Agent. No prior SEO knowledge is required.
- Automated SEO Planning: Flavio continually monitors your website, identifies areas for improvement, and generates a clear roadmap of recommended SEO adjustments.
- Visibility Enhancement: By identifying key foundational SEO issues, Flavio helps prepare your site to be easily found by search engines, boosting your potential search ranking.
- Clear Progress Updates: Understand your SEO health with straightforward, easy-to-read progress reports. Flavio translates complex SEO data into actionable insights, showing you the issues we've found and the steps you can take.
- Focus on Your Business: You concentrate on what you do best—running your business—and let Flavio handle the technical details of improving your online presence.

Access and Versions

Flavio is designed to give you immediate value while offering the flexibility to upgrade.

Limited Version (Free)
- Automated Technical Fixes - Auditing Only (Identifies issues; fixes are disabled)
- In-Depth Reporting - Summary Only
- Priority Support - Not Included

 Full Access (30 Day Trial)

- Core SEO Audit & Reporting - Included
- Automated Technical Fixes - Automated Fixes Enabled (Flavio automatically executes improvements)
- In-Depth Reporting - Full Analytics and Insights
- Priority Support - Included

Start with a generous 30-day period of full access to all features—no credit card required—to experience the full power of Flavio, including automated fixing. After the trial, the plugin seamlessly transitions to the Limited Version, which continues to provide essential auditing and planning of your site's SEO health (showing you what needs to be fixed and what was already done). You may then choose to upgrade at any time to re-enable automated fixes and access premium features.
Flavio works with the marketgoo platform to analyze and improve your website. You’ll need a free marketgoo account to activate it.

Flavio handles the SEO. You handle the business.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the `flavio` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to the Flavio menu in your WordPress admin dashboard
4. Follow the setup wizard to connect your site

**Minimum Requirements:**

* WordPress 5.0 or greater
* PHP version 7.4 or greater
* MySQL version 5.6 or greater

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Flavio? =

Flavio is an AI-powered SEO assistant plugin for WordPress that helps you optimize your site with automated tasks and intelligent recommendations.

= Do I need technical SEO knowledge to use Flavio? =

No. Flavio is designed to make SEO accessible to everyone. It provides clear explanations and automates complex tasks.

= Will Flavio conflict with my existing SEO plugin? =

Flavio is designed to work alongside other SEO plugins. However, we recommend reviewing specific features to avoid duplicating functionality like sitemap generation.

= How does the sitemap generator work? =

Flavio automatically generates an XML sitemap that includes all your published posts, pages, custom post types, categories, and tags. It can be regenerated at any time from the plugin dashboard.

= Can I customize the robots.txt file? =

The current version generates an optimized robots.txt file with SEO best practices. Manual customization can be done by editing the file directly in your WordPress root directory.

= How does the broken links fixer work? =

Flavio scans your content for broken internal and external links (404 errors), then provides options to automatically remove or replace them.

= Is Flavio free? =

Please visit our website for current pricing information and available plans.

= Where can I get support? =

For support, please visit the plugin support forum on WordPress.org or contact us through our website.
